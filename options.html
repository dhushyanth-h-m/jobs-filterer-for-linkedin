You can:

  • type Alt-S or Option-S to save options (equivalent to clicking the Save button);
  • write a regexp like “/regexp/i” to make it case-insensitive;
  • prefix a regexp with "t:" in any of the preceding text boxes to designate it as a title regexp (so you don't have to keep moving between text fields);
  • "c:" same as above but for company regexps; and
  • "l:" same as above but for location regexps.
